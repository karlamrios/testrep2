Ahead of class, things to do...

In order to be successful these are some things you can do to to stay on top.
  • Dedicate some time to learn new things.
  • Stick to the things you are assigned, don't get fancy by default.
  • Make sure to use notes you take to review
  • You have until Friday to decide if you want to stay. Really think about it
Things to remember to do while you are in the classroom.
  • Write down the assignments because they don't stay up long enough.
  • Assume you are only there to get notes.
  • Remember to charge your laptop every day.
